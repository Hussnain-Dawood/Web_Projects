Our Story

From a Single Store to a National Icon

TechHub Electronics began in a modest shopfront on George Street, Sydney, founded by two tech-obsessed mates — Marcus Whitfield and Priya Nair — who believed Australians deserved more than confusing salespeople and overpriced electronics.

Their philosophy was radical for the time: transparent pricing, knowledgeable staff who actually use the products they sell, and a genuine commitment to after-sales support. It resonated immediately.

Today, TechHub is Australia's most awarded electronics retailer — but we haven't forgotten those roots. Every decision we make comes back to the same question our founders asked in 1998: "Would we be proud to sell this to a friend?"

Marcus Whitfield and Priya Nair open the first TechHub store on George Street, Sydney, with a team of five.

What Drives Us

Our Core Values

Everything we do at TechHub is guided by a simple set of principles that haven't changed since Day 1.

Radical Transparency

No hidden fees, no fine print tricks, no commission-driven upsells. Our prices are our prices — and our staff are trained to recommend what's right for you, not what earns them the most.

Genuine Expertise

Every TechHub team member completes our in-house Tech Academy — a 6-week immersive training program. We don't just sell gadgets; we understand them inside and out.

Customer First, Always

Our 30-day hassle-free returns, price-match guarantee, and lifetime customer support aren't marketing slogans — they're commitments baked into how we operate every single day.

Sustainable Future

Our e-waste recycling program, carbon-neutral shipping initiative, and commitment to 100% renewable energy in all stores by 2028 reflect our responsibility to Australia's environment.

Community Roots

Each of our 48 stores has a dedicated Community Tech Fund, sponsoring local schools, coding bootcamps, and digital literacy programs for seniors across Australia.

Always Innovating

From Australia's first Try Before You Buy program to same-day delivery and AI-powered recommendations, TechHub never stops looking for better ways to serve our customers.
