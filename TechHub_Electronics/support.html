Frequently Asked Questions

Once your order is dispatched, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking link. You can also log into your profile at any time and view live tracking under "Order History". Same-day orders in eligible cities are tracked via our courier partner's real-time map.

Standard shipping takes 2–5 business days Australia-wide. Express shipping is 1–2 business days. Same-day delivery is available in Sydney, Melbourne and Brisbane for orders placed before 12pm AEST. Remote areas may take up to 10 business days. Click & Collect is ready in as little as 2 hours at your nearest store.

Orders can be cancelled or modified within 1 hour of placement — log into your profile and select "Edit Order". After this window, the order will be in fulfilment and can no longer be changed. Once received, you can initiate a return under our 30-day policy.

We offer a hassle-free 30-day return policy on all products. Items must be in original condition with all accessories and original packaging. Software, digital downloads, and opened headphones/earbuds are excluded for hygiene reasons. Returns are free — simply print the prepaid label from your account or drop it at any store.

Once your return is received and inspected, refunds are processed within 3–5 business days. Credit card refunds typically appear within 2–7 business days depending on your bank. PayPal refunds are usually instant. Afterpay refunds are applied to your outstanding balance immediately.

All products come with the manufacturer's standard warranty plus your Australian Consumer Law rights. To make a claim, bring your item and proof of purchase to any TechHub store, or submit a claim via your online profile. Our in-store Tech Bar team can diagnose the issue on the spot — most repairs are completed within 5 business days.

TechHub Care is our optional extended protection plan — available for most products at checkout. It extends your manufacturer warranty by 2 additional years and adds accidental damage cover. TechHub Care members get priority in-store repair service and a free loan device for repairs over 3 business days.

You earn 1 point for every $1 spent. Points can be redeemed at $0.01 per point (e.g., 500 points = $5 off). Points expire after 24 months of account inactivity. Gold members (5,000+ lifetime points) earn 1.5x points on all purchases. Platinum members (20,000+ lifetime points) earn 2x points and get free express shipping on every order.

Click "Forgot Password?" on the Sign In page and enter your registered email address. You'll receive a reset link within 2 minutes. If you don't see it, check your spam folder. If you're still having trouble, our support team can verify your identity and reset your account manually.

We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Afterpay (buy now, pay later in 4 fortnightly instalments). TechHub Rewards points can also be applied at checkout. We do not accept cryptocurrency or direct bank transfers for online orders.

Payments can be declined for several reasons: insufficient funds, incorrect card details, or your bank flagging an unusual transaction. Try re-entering your card details, contacting your bank to authorise the charge, or using an alternative payment method. Your order will not be placed until payment is successfully processed.
