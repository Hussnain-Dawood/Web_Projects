Returns & Exchanges

We want you to love every Vogue Vista piece. If something isn’t quite right, our returns policy makes it easy to send items back.

Return Eligibility

You can return most full-priced items within 30 days of delivery for a refund or exchange, provided they meet the conditions below.

  • Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original condition.
  • All original tags and labels must be attached.
  • Items must be free from makeup, perfume, or other marks.
  • Proof of purchase (order confirmation or receipt) is required.
Note: For hygiene reasons, earrings, undergarments, and final sale items are not eligible for change-of-mind returns unless they are faulty under Australian Consumer Law.

How to Start a Return

To initiate a return, please follow these steps:

  • Visit the Contact Us page and select “Returns & Exchanges”.
  • Provide your order number, item name, and the reason for return.
  • Our team will review your request and email you return instructions.
  • Pack your item securely and attach the provided return label (if applicable).

Once your parcel reaches our warehouse, we’ll inspect the items and notify you via email when your refund or exchange has been processed.

Refunds

Approved returns will be refunded to your original payment method, excluding any shipping charges paid at checkout (unless the item is confirmed faulty).

  • Refunds are usually processed within 3–5 business days after inspection.
  • Your bank or payment provider may require additional processing time.
  • Gift card purchases will be refunded as store credit.

Exchanges

If you’d like to exchange an item for a different size or colour, please specify this in your return request. Exchanges are subject to stock availability at the time we process your parcel.

If your preferred item is no longer available, we’ll process a refund instead so you can choose an alternative style.

Faulty or Incorrect Items

We take quality seriously. If you receive a faulty, damaged, or incorrect item, please contact us as soon as possible with photos and your order number.

Where a product is deemed faulty, we will provide a replacement, repair, or refund in line with Australian Consumer Law.

At a Glance

  • 30-day change-of-mind returns on full-priced items.
  • Items must be unworn, unwashed, and with tags.
  • No returns on earrings, intimates, or final sale (unless faulty).
  • Refunds processed to original payment method.
