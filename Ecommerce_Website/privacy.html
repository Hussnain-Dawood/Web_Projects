Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains how Vogue Vista collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

Last updated: 01 December 2025  |  Applies to all visitors and customers of the Vogue Vista website.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you interact with our site:

  • Contact details such as name, email address, phone number, and delivery address.
  • Account information such as login credentials and wishlist preferences.
  • Order and payment details (processed securely via our payment partners).
  • Usage data such as pages viewed, clicks, and device information (via cookies and analytics tools).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process and deliver your orders and provide customer support.
  • Manage your account, including your wishlist and order history.
  • Send you service-related messages such as order confirmations and updates.
  • Send marketing emails and style updates where you have opted in (you can unsubscribe at any time).
  • Improve our website, products, and overall shopping experience.

3. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to remember your preferences, keep you signed in, and understand how visitors use our site.

You can adjust your browser settings to refuse cookies, but this may impact certain features such as keeping items in your cart.

4. Sharing Your Data

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited data with trusted third parties who help us deliver our services, including:

  • Payment processors to handle secure transactions.
  • Shipping carriers to deliver your orders.
  • Email service providers to send newsletters and order updates.
  • Analytics tools to help us understand site performance and user behaviour.

These partners are required to protect your data and use it only for the services they provide to us.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorised access, disclosure, or misuse.

While we work hard to safeguard your data, no method of transmission or storage can be guaranteed 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Request deletion of certain information, subject to legal obligations.
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time.

7. Retention

We keep your personal information only for as long as necessary to provide our services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page with an updated date.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about how we handle your personal data, please reach out via the Contact Us page.
