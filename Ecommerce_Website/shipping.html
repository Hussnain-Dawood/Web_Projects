Shipping & Delivery

From our Sydney warehouse to your wardrobe — here’s how shipping works for Vogue Vista orders.

Shipping Within Australia

We partner with trusted carriers to deliver orders across Australia quickly and reliably. Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on your location and selected service.

Service Estimated Time From (AUD)
Standard Delivery 3–7 business days $7.95
Express Delivery 1–3 business days $14.95
Click & Collect (selected stores) 2–5 business days Free
Free shipping may be offered during selected promotions or when your order exceeds a minimum spend threshold. Check the banner on our Home page for current offers.

International Shipping

Vogue Vista currently ships to selected international destinations. Available countries and exact charges will appear at checkout once you enter your delivery address.

  • Delivery times vary by region (usually 7–14 business days).
  • Local customs duties and taxes may apply and are the responsibility of the customer.
  • Certain items may be restricted due to local regulations or carrier limitations.

Order Tracking

As soon as your order leaves our warehouse, you’ll receive a shipping confirmation email containing your tracking number and a link to follow your parcel.

You can also log into your account at any time to review your order history and check the latest tracking updates.

Delays & Delivery Issues

Occasionally, unexpected events such as severe weather, public holidays, or carrier disruptions may cause delays. We’ll communicate any significant changes by email where possible.

If your parcel appears delayed or missing, please contact our support team with your order number so we can investigate with the carrier.

Delivery Tips

  • Double-check your address at checkout to avoid delays.
  • Use an address where someone will be available to accept the delivery, such as your workplace or a secure parcel locker.
  • Keep an eye on your tracking link for real-time updates.
  • For time-sensitive occasions, choose Express Delivery or order a few days earlier than needed.

For more detail about returns and refunds for delivered orders, see our Returns & Exchanges page.
