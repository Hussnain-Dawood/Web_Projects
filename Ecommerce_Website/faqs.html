Orders & Payments

Everything you need to know about placing and managing your order.

Which payment methods do you accept?

We currently accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and PayPal for online purchases. All payments are processed securely in AUD.

Can I modify or cancel my order after placing it?

We start preparing your order as soon as it is confirmed. If you need to change or cancel, please contact us within 1 hour of placing the order and we’ll do our best to help.

Will I be charged customs or duties?

Orders within Australia are not charged customs duties. For international deliveries, local customs or import taxes may apply depending on your country’s regulations.

Shipping & Delivery

Timelines, tracking, and delivery options for your Vogue Vista order.

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery within Australia typically takes 3–7 business days, depending on your location. Express options are available at checkout for faster delivery.

How can I track my order?

Once your parcel leaves our warehouse, you’ll receive a shipping confirmation email with a tracking link. You can check real-time updates any time.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship to selected international destinations. Available countries and estimated timelines are shown on the Shipping page and at checkout.

Returns & Refunds

Learn how returns work and when you can expect your refund.

What is your return policy?

We accept change-of-mind returns within 30 days of delivery for full-priced items that are unworn, unwashed, and in original condition with tags attached. View full details on our Returns page.

How do I initiate a return?

You can submit a return request using the instructions on the Returns page. After it’s approved, you’ll receive a prepaid label or drop-off instructions, depending on your location.

When will I receive my refund?

Once we’ve received and inspected your return, refunds are typically processed within 3–5 business days back to your original payment method. Processing times may vary by bank.

Accounts & Wishlist

Information about your Vogue Vista profile and saved items.

Do I need an account to place an order?

You can checkout as a guest, but we recommend creating an account for faster checkout, order tracking, and saving your wishlist.

How does the wishlist work?

Add items to your wishlist from any product page. When you sign in, your wishlist is synced across devices, so you can access it anytime.

How is my personal data used?

We only use your data to provide and improve your shopping experience. Read more in our Privacy Policy.
